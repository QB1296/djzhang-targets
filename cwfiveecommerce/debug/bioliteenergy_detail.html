Inside The Tech

Inside our Technology

Watch: Inside The Tech

Big Light in a Small Space

  1. Edge-lighting: A unique textured pattern on the lens reflects the LEDs on a steep angle creating soft, even light along the entire panel without requiring a cavernous space typical of most lanterns.

  2. High-efficiency LEDs: Red and white LEDs are located at the top of the design providing 4 key lighting modes.

  3. Rechargeable Battery: A 1350 mAh battery maximizes burn time and gives the ability to amplify the lumen output with a front-panel point light. USB-charge out gives you the ability to provide a power boost to your gear when on the go.

  4. Stainless Steel Housing: Thinner and even more durable than plastic, the stainless steel housing works double time as a heat sink, helping the LEDs run more efficiently.
